Sri Lanka Electoral Assembly on Development
A novel conception in Sri Lankan MUN, SLEAD is not your typical committee. It is an extraordinary committee designed to hit home and opens up an arena to discuss issues that are specific to Sri Lanka. Like UNESCO it is to be trilingual so that anyone might be given the opportunity to air their views. It will consist of delegates representing the electorates of Sri Lanka. They are to represent the needs and requirements of the people of their respective electorates in respect to handling the various developmental and constitutional challenges faced by a Sri Lanka in transition. In our venture to Shifting Policy Paradigms, we have ventured into shifting the paradigms of MUN itself through the inception of SLEAD. Delegates will be allocated electorates with respect to their school’s respective electorate. They will be challenged towards creating their own policies based on limited resources and local knowledge. Unlike other committees, SLEAD will appeal to the very Sri Lankans hearts and minds that SLMUN aims to revolutionize. This committee will provide a pedestal for the youth of all arts of Sri Lanka to actually speak out for what they believe is necessary for the development of their respective electorate and the country as a whole. Hence we look forward to a novel experience through this novel conception.
